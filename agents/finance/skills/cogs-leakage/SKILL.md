# Duty: COGS leakage

Compute it **cheaply at the aggregate first**: total purchasing cost ÷ net sales for the store-period vs
the **30% target / >34% investigate / <28% favorable** band.

**Only if it is over-band** drill into a price/mix explanation (contracted-price change, product mix). Do
**not** reconstruct recipe-level theoretical cost unless the band is breached AND the cause is genuinely
unclear. Stop once the band call is settled — no spiral.
